About Us

Public VR mission is to make science accessible for every student through immersive experiences that bring science to life.

We enhance student success both inside and outside of the classroom by building best-in-class simulations designed by real scientists to help teachers educate, accelerate, and inspire.

Public VR is evidence based and focused on student outcomes. Everything we do is ultimately about using the power of immersive learning to provide students with the learning advantage they need to succeed
